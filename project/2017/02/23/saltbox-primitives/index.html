2017 Saltbox Primitives project

Saltbox Primitives Maine provides New England style home decor and furniture. Nearly 10 years later, I decided to redesign their website utlizing Shopify. Yes, I know its not Drupal but they needed a solution that could also connect with the Point of Sale system to manage all of their inventory. One of the challenges they had, would be something sold on the website but it would be out of stock.

Of course I couldn't just purchase any old theme, so I dove into the template system. They use the templating system liquid wich was developed by Jon Snoop. You know the famous SMACCS creator. In the end, I added a few customizations to limit the purchase amount to $25.
